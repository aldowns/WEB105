Gaming

I love cozy gaming!

I love to play games that provide an outlet for my own creativity.

Stardew Valley

The definition of cozy gaming, Stardew Valley, is one of my favorite games. For 15 dollars I have gotten over 500 hours of playtime on my PC and I was happy to purchase it again for both my phone and my Switch.

It is not as customizable as Animal Crossing as it is a farming simulator at its core. But the relaxed, go-at-your-own-pace nature of the game keeps me coming back for more.

Animal Crossing New Horizons

There are many outlets for creativity within Animal Crossing. I've played the series since I was five, starting with the original game on Gamecube. New Horizons for the Nintendo Switch gave me many tools to create clothes and decorate my own world.

Although I found the character dialogue lacking compared to other games, the improved customization abilities give me a reason to go back to it every now and again; even when I prefer the charm of the older games.
