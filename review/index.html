Cassette Beasts

Cassette Beasts is an indie creature-collector RPG that was released last year on Steam, Nintendo Switch, and Xbox. The game features a monster-filled island that many individuals had washed up on for centuries. With a cassette player in hand, it is up to the player character to find a way back to reality and solve the mystery by recording monster forms to fight through the dangerous creatures on the island.

Story

The game begins with the player washing up on the shore of a dangerous mysterious island called New Wirral. For centuries, hundreds of people have begun to wash up on this island all from varying places and time periods. No one has ever been able to leave the island as all attempts are supernaturally interrupted. You’re given a cassette player, which allows you to transform into the various monster forms around the islands. You’re given many side quests and missions as with many RPGs but your main goal is to solve the mystery of the island and get everyone back home.

Kayleigh

Kayleigh is the first character you meet on the island. She acts as the tutorial for the player and accompanies you for most of your journey. She's a bright and friendly character who makes New Wirral feel like your home.
